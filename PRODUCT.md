# Product

## Register

product

## Users

BeeNut is used by operators and technicians running a dedicated object-counting kiosk on desktop machines, Raspberry Pi, or single-board computers. Users work near live camera hardware, often with touch input, and need fast target selection, clear counts, preview inspection, settings, diagnostics, and recovery paths.

## Product Purpose

BeeNut turns a machine into a reliable computer-vision counting appliance for custom YOLO ONNX models. Success means the operator can see the live preview, trust the count state, choose or configure targets, and recover from hardware or model issues without leaving the kiosk workflow.

## Brand Personality

Practical, precise, and field-ready. The interface should feel calm, durable, and direct rather than decorative.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site flourishes, over-rounded consumer app styling, decorative motion, hidden controls, low-contrast diagnostics, and any visual treatment that competes with the live preview or count status.

## Design Principles

- Put the counting task first.
- Make hardware and model state legible at a glance.
- Keep touch targets obvious and consistent.
- Prefer durable industrial clarity over softness or ornament.
- Preserve operator confidence during errors, loading, and degraded hardware states.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain high contrast, clear focus states, touch-friendly hit areas, readable Thai and Latin typography through the bundled Noto Sans fonts, and reduced-motion-friendly interactions.
